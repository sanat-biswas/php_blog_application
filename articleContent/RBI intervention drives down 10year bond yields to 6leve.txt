<p style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: 'open sans', Arial; font-size: 15px; background-color: #ffffff; line-height: 24px !important; padding: 2px 0px !important; margin: 0px 0px 12px !important;"><span style="font-family: open sans, Arial;"><span style="font-size: 15px;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>

While completely leaving out a bond maturing in 2028, and buying Rs 2,040 crore and Rs 3,306 crore in the 2024 maturity and 2034 maturity respectively.</span></span></p>

The 10-year bond yields closed at 6.0096 per cent.</span></span></p>
